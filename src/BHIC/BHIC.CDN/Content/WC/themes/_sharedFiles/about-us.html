We promise to make the handling of your claims a top priority.

We understand you purchase insurance because of the real possibility of a loss. If that happens, you want to be sure you have the resources (financial and otherwise) needed to experience a full recovery as quickly as possible. When you need us, you want us to be available and to have the experience and expertise to deal effectively with the situation. Our toll-free number connects you to us fast and allows us to begin working on the claim right away. During that initial call, you’ll find that:

  • Providing any needed medical care is always our first priority, and we have access to a national network of preferred health care providers to expedite that process as well as nurses on hand to help develop a sound treatment plan.
  • Securing evidence is important before the opportunity is lost, so we’ll remind you to collect the contact information of any witnesses and perhaps take photos of the scene.
  • Protecting any damaged property against further damage is also critical, and we can provide some basic tips on steps you can take before our adjusters arrive on the scene (for Business Owner’s Policies).

We pride ourselves on our high standards of customer service throughout the course of the claim from first report to final resolution . . . and we recognize that frequent communication among all parties is essential to a good outcome.
